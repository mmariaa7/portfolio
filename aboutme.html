Who am I?

I am Maria Morozova, a creative and detail-oriented web designer and UI/UX specialist currently pursuing a Master's in Human-Computer Interaction. I have a strong background in front-end development and digital design, and I'm particularly interested in creating user-friendly and aesthetically pleasing digital experiences.

My work ranges from designing intuitive interfaces for industrial platforms to experimenting with innovative interactions like tangible geospatial systems. I strive to bridge the gap between functionality and visual storytelling in every project I take on.

I love working with design tools like Figma and Adobe Creative Suite, and I bring my designs to life through the programming languages I’ve mastered during my academic and professional journey.

My research experience allowed me to explore existing innovations and develop my own vision to create new things.
