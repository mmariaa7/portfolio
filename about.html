Interactive Prototype

As part of our final-year Master's project, our team collaborated with Airbus on a design challenge. We developed an intuitive, digital version of their avionic options catalogue to enhance commercial meetings. The result: a high-fidelity, interactive prototype now being used as the base for the final system.

Work done

  • 18 interviews with Airbus teams
  • Co-creation workshops & ideation sessions
  • 4 low-fidelity prototypes
  • 2 mid-fidelity prototypes
  • 1 high-fidelity Figma prototype

Results

  • Validated and approved by Airbus
  • Used to initiate development of the final tool
